Viết chương trình (Timer)

- Cho blink led trong 1s không dùng delay (sử dụng Ngắt và Timer0, Timer1)	(OK)
- Chạy thời gian đếm hiển thị lên LCD, đơn vị 1s (cho Timer0 và Timer1)		(OK)

Ex: PIC16F877A
	Timer0: 8-bit timer/counter with 8-bit prescaler
	Timer1: 16-bit timer/counter with prescaler, can be incremented during Sleep via external crystal/clock
	Timer2: 8-bit timer/counter with 8-bit period register, prescaler and postscaler

Hardware Interrupts
	External interrupt
	Interrupt-on-change pin (IOC)

Software Interrupts
	Timer0 and its interrupt
	Timer1 and its interrupt
	Timer2 and its interrupt
